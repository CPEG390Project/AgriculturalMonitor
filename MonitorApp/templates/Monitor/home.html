Here at the Agricultural Monitor we help you help the plants you love stay healthy and happy!

Like humans, plants needs specific temperatures and environmental factors to be healthy! But while humans can move away or cover up when they are cold, plants need a little extra help. So for example, the ideal climate for a blushing roses is 27C with a relative humidity of 60-70%, while the delicate Dahlias' require humid environments with temperatures ranging from 20 to 22C in the morning and 15 to 17 in the night! We offer you three choices: A generalized test where you can input your own maximum temperature, and two default plants.
